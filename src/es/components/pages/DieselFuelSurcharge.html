Diesel fuel surcharge

Since 2008 FMC Logistics Transport has been granting its transport partners a diesel fuel surcharge in addition to the agreed transport rates. This surcharge is indexed and is based on the changes in the fuel market. The resulting increases or reductions in costs are credited or charged on a one-to-one Basis to the customer by FMC Logistics Transport one to one.

Basis
The amount of the surcharge or discount is based on the current diesel prices at the fuel pump of ASTAG.

CALCULATION EXAMPLE

The fuel surcharge/discount is recalculatet every month according to the following principle:
